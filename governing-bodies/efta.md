# EFTA

## Acronyms

| Acronym | Backronym |
| - | - |
| EFTA | European Free Trade Association |
| EU | European Union |

## Overview

Switzerland is not technically a member of the EU. Instead, it is a member of a four-nation smaller body known as the EFTA along with Iceland, Lichtenstein, and Norway.
